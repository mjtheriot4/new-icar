We Analyse and Visualize Social Media Data

Understanding the sources, patterns, and consequences of social and geographical disparities in disaster resilience is critical to building long-term resilient, healthy, and sustainable communities. Traditional resilience analysis has been confined mostly to using static data collected at scheduled intervals. With the advent of the Big Data era, real-time human response data extracted from social media could provide new opportunities for studying disparities in disaster resilience.
